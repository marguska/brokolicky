Welcome kofí lovers&addicts!
We're here to help you find the ideal coffee for your tastebuds

As a founding Certified B Corporation and specialty coffee reviewer, Kofí has connected roasters, growers, and coffee drinkers since 2018. Our offerings range from light to dark roasted coffees, focusing on balance and sweetness (or whatever you taste, because let's be honest, it's all personally relative). We are here to learn, discover coffee with you, and not take ourselves too seriously. Bottoms Up!
